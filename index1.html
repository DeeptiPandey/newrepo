TECHNOLOGY

Technology! The word in itself probably bears a plethora of components. Literally, the word just means application of scientific knowledge in practical purposes, but on grounds it may have somewhat different meanings to it. For most of the people round the globe, technology might refer to their cellular devices and such stuff. As for me, technology resides in as simple as a matchstick to artificial intelligence. Infact the way I define technology is like each and everything you see around, that isn't nature's work, is a result of some kind of technology.
